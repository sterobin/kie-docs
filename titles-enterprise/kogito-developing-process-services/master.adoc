= Developing process services in {PRODUCT}
:context: developing-process-services
:imagesdir: _images

As a developer of business processes, you can use {PRODUCT} to develop process services and case definitions using Business Process Model and Notation (BPMN) 2.0 models. BPMN process models are graphical representations of the steps required to achieve a business goal. For more information about BPMN, see the Object Management Group (OMG) https://www.omg.org/spec/BPMN/2.0/About-BPMN[Business Process Model and Notation 2.0 specification].
